What is the Tech Trader Token?

     Last year, a startup emerged having created one of the first fully autonomous hedge funds managed entirely by machine. The technology behind it is called Tech Trader, and it has been trading live capital for over 4 years with no human intervention. It is fully automated trading in the truest sense, unlike many "automated" or "AI" systems which may have a hundred engineers providing the actual intelligence behind the curtain. To this day, Tech Trader continues to run with no tweaking or updates by anyone, even posting to its own blog and social media accounts under the handle @TechTraderAI. Now the team behind that endeavor is taking things even further by having the fund itself offered on the blockchain, vastly reducing what little human involvement was left in the business.

     The Tech Trader Token is a fully autonomous hedge fund issued as a blockchain-based token. The token will be unlike most before it in that each unit individually tracks a corresponding dollar of value in the fund, regardless of how many tokens are issued. It is a token backed one-to-one by the real assets and performance of a live already operating business, instead of having its value driven by speculation or scarcity of supply. The token will be offered with an initial soft cap of $100M to eligible non-US investors and be immediately tradable on Ripple's built-in decentralized exchange, in essence also creating a hedge fund with 24/7 tradability and liquidity.

How to Participate in the Token Sale

     The Tech Trader Token will be issued directly on Ripple and only be accessible to eligible non-US investors who have completed our registration process. Due to securities laws, registration is broken into two steps where you must first pass KYC/AML before you can view the fund details and complete the final subscription document. Once registered, you will be able to buy and sell the Tech Trader Token directly on the Ripple Consensus Ledger (Ripple's blockchain), which is actually also decentralized exchange. You must have a Ripple account and use a full-functionality Ripple wallet software to access it. We recommend TheWorldExchange.net, which, despite the name, is just a free interface that will allow you to create a wallet, fund it, and buy or sell anything directly off of Ripple.

  1. Create and fund a Ripple account using TheWorldExchange.net or other wallet software. See tutorial here.
  2. Register to be screened for KYC/AML.
  3. Once you pass KYC/AML, you will have access to fund details and subscription form.
  4. Once subscription is submitted, add the token to your Trustlines / Receivable Tokens (tutorial here). You will then be whitelisted to buy or sell it on your Ripple account (tutorial here).
